# Rice Cooker

## What It Is

An electric appliance that heats rice and water, detects when free water has been absorbed or evaporated, and then switches to warming.

## Best Uses

- Rice
- Mixed grains
- Porridge
- Hands-off steaming, when supported

## Capabilities

Programs vary by model and may account for rice type, soaking, cooking, steaming, and resting.

## Limitations

- Cup markings may use the smaller rice-cooker cup rather than a standard US cup.
- Results depend on rice variety, rinsing, soaking, and water ratio.
- Keep-warm can gradually dry or discolor rice.

## Safety

- Keep steam vents clear.
- Avoid touching the lid or escaping steam during cooking.
- Keep the heating plate and outside of the inner pot dry before use.

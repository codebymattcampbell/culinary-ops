# Microwave

## What It Is

An appliance that uses microwave energy to heat water-rich foods quickly throughout part of their volume rather than only from the surface.

## Best Uses

- Reheating
- Melting and softening
- Steaming small portions
- Rapidly heating liquids

## Capabilities

Power levels usually cycle the magnetron on and off rather than continuously reducing output. Lower settings can therefore improve heating uniformity by allowing time for heat to spread.

## Limitations

- Heating can be uneven.
- Browning is minimal without specialized cookware.
- Dense or irregular foods may develop hot and cold spots.

## Safety

- Use microwave-safe containers.
- Vent covered foods and open containers away from the face.
- Stir liquids and allow standing time before consuming.
- Never heat sealed containers or metal unless the manufacturer explicitly permits it.

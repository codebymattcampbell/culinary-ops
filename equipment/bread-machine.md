# Bread Machine

## What It Is

A countertop appliance that mixes, kneads, proofs, and bakes bread in a removable pan using programmed cycles.

## Best Uses

- Consistent sandwich loaves
- Enriched breads
- Dough mixing and proofing
- Low-effort bread production

## Capabilities

Capabilities vary by model but commonly include loaf-size settings, crust settings, delayed starts, dough-only cycles, and specialized bread programs.

## Limitations

- Fixed pan shape and loaf geometry
- Kneading paddles leave holes in the bottom of the loaf
- Program timing may not suit every dough
- Small ingredient changes can affect rise and pan capacity

## Safety

- Keep hands and utensils out of the pan while the machine is running.
- Use heat protection when removing the hot pan.
- Keep vents clear and place the machine on a stable, heat-resistant surface.
- Disconnect power before cleaning.

## Care and Maintenance

- Allow the pan to cool before washing.
- Do not immerse the machine body in water.
- Dry the pan and paddles completely before storage.
- Check paddle shafts and seals for residue or wear.

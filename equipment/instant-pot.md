# Instant Pot

## What It Is

An electric multicooker that can pressure cook, slow cook, steam, sauté, and keep food warm.

## Best Uses

- Beans and grains
- Braises and stews
- Stocks
- Fast tenderization of tough cuts

## Capabilities

Pressure cooking raises the boiling point of water, allowing moist cooking at temperatures above normal atmospheric boiling.

## Limitations

- It does not brown food while sealed.
- Liquid reduction is limited during pressure cooking.
- Total cook time includes pressurization and pressure release.

## Safety

- Keep the steam-release path clear.
- Never force the lid open while pressurized.
- Avoid overfilling, especially with foaming foods.
- Inspect the sealing ring and valves before use.

# Equipment

Equipment guides explain what a tool does, what it is capable of, how to use it safely, and what we have learned about the specific equipment in our kitchen.

Recipes remain in `recipes/`. A recipe lists and links its required equipment rather than being reorganized or duplicated here.

Each guide separates broadly useful information from **Our Equipment Notes**, where we record model-specific behavior, quirks, maintenance, and lessons learned through actual cooks.

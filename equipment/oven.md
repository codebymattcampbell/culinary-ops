# Oven

## What It Is

An enclosed cooking chamber that heats food with hot air, radiant heat, or both.

## Best Uses

- Baking
- Roasting
- Broiling
- Drying and reheating

## Capabilities

Most home ovens support low-temperature warming through high-temperature roasting and broiling. Convection models also circulate air to increase heat transfer at the food's surface.

## Limitations

- Displayed temperature may differ from the actual chamber temperature.
- Rack position and hot spots affect browning.
- A preheat signal does not always mean the walls, racks, and cookware are fully stabilized.

## Safety

- Use dry oven mitts.
- Keep combustible items away from heating elements.
- Confirm cookware is oven-safe.
- Do not line the oven floor with foil unless the manufacturer explicitly allows it.

# Collections

Collections are curated ways to discover recipes based on the situation rather than the food's primary category.

A recipe has one canonical home under `recipes/`. It may appear in several collections without duplicating the recipe itself.

## Purpose

- `recipes/` answers: **How do I make this dish?**
- `experiments/` answers: **What happened when we cooked it, and what should change?**
- `collections/` answers: **What should I cook for this need or constraint?**

## Collection Guidelines

- Link to the canonical recipe instead of copying instructions.
- Group recipes by useful decision criteria such as time, cleanup, budget, equipment, or occasion.
- A recipe may belong to multiple collections.
- Keep collection descriptions brief and practical.

- [Quick Meals](quick-meals.md): Recipes selected for low effort, short active time, or fast service.

# Baby Back Ribs Experiments

## Current Recipe

See `recipes/pork/baby-back-ribs.md`.

## Objective

Develop an Egg method that produces even doneness across the full rack without overcooking the thin ends.

## Experiment History

- [2026-06 first cook](2026-06-first-cook.md)

## Current Lessons

- The center of the rack cooked better than the thin ends.
- Thin sections need protection, earlier removal, or separate doneness checks.
- Pit temperature control needs to remain closer to the target.

# Chuck Roast v1

## Objective
Create a steak-like chuck roast using sous vide and high-heat stainless steel searing.

## Status
In progress

## Equipment
- Sous vide
- 12 inch Viking stainless steel pan
- Blackstone griddle as heat source
- IR thermometer

## Variables
- Beef: 1.9 lb boneless chuck roast
- Thickness: about 2 inches
- Sous vide temperature: 137°F
- Sous vide time: 20 hours
- Sear target: 550°F pan preheat, expected drop to 500-525°F
- Oil: avocado oil
- Finish: butter, garlic, herbs

## Timeline
- Bagged: 10:00 PM
- Target pull: 5:35-5:40 PM
- Fridge dry: 20 minutes
- Sear: about 6:00 PM

## Hypothesis
137°F for 20 hours will produce a steak-like texture with softened connective tissue, while a dried surface and hot stainless pan will create a strong crust without overcooking the interior.

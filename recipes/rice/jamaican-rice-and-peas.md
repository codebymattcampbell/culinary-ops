# Jamaican Rice and Peas

A coconut-scented rice and bean side for jerk pork and other Caribbean-style mains.

## Status

**Experimental**

## Yield

To be determined during testing.

## Required Equipment

- [Instant Pot](../../equipment/instant-pot.md) or stovetop pot

## Ingredients

- Brown rice or white rice
- Kidney beans or pigeon peas
- Coconut milk
- Scallions
- Garlic
- Fresh thyme
- Allspice
- Bay leaf
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Water, as needed

## Method

1. Cook the aromatics briefly.
2. Add the rice, beans, coconut milk, water, thyme, allspice, bay leaf, salt, and pepper.
3. Cook until the rice is tender.
4. Rest covered before fluffing.

## Quality Targets

- Separate, tender grains rather than a wet or gummy texture
- Coconut flavor present but not overpowering
- Beans intact and evenly distributed

## Notes

- White rice is more traditional, but brown rice can be tested for family preference.
- Exact ratios and cooking times still need validation for both stovetop and Instant Pot methods.

# Ethiopian Cabbage (Atakilt Wat)

A gently spiced cabbage, carrot, and potato side with turmeric, ginger, and garlic.

## Status

**Experimental**

## Yield

Approximately 6 servings.

## Required Equipment

- Dutch oven or large sauté pan
- [Stovetop](../../equipment/stovetop.md)
- Cutting board
- Chef's knife

## Ingredients

- Green cabbage, sliced
- Carrots, sliced
- Yukon Gold potatoes, diced
- Yellow onion, sliced
- Garlic, minced
- Fresh ginger, minced or grated
- Turmeric
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Neutral oil or olive oil

## Method

1. Cook the onion until softened.
2. Add the garlic, ginger, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
3. Add the carrots and potatoes and cook briefly.
4. Add the cabbage and cook until tender but not mushy.
5. Adjust the salt and serve warm.

## Quality Targets

- Sweet, tender cabbage that still has structure
- Potatoes fully tender
- Warm turmeric and ginger aroma without raw spice flavor

## Notes

- Reduce the potatoes when the meal already includes bread or rice.
- Exact quantities and timing still need validation.

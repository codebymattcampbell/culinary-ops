# Lemon Herb Yogurt

A bright, cooling yogurt sauce for braised lamb, spicy meats, flatbread, roasted vegetables, and cabbage dishes.

## Status

**Experimental**

## Yield

Approximately 6 servings.

## Required Equipment

- Mixing bowl
- Microplane or fine grater

## Ingredients

- Plain Greek yogurt
- Lemon zest
- Lemon juice
- Garlic, finely grated
- Fresh mint, chopped
- Cilantro or parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Stir the yogurt with lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper.
2. Rest for 10–20 minutes when possible.
3. Taste and adjust the salt and lemon before serving.

## Quality Targets

- Bright and cooling
- Noticeable lemon without becoming sharply acidic
- Loose enough to spoon but not watery

## Notes

- This is not intended to be tzatziki.
- Its role is to cool and brighten a plate without pulling the meal fully into Greek flavors.
- Exact ingredient quantities still need validation.

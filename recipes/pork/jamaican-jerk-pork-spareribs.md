# Jamaican Jerk Pork Spareribs

Pork spareribs built around jerk flavors rather than a ketchup-heavy barbecue sauce.

## Status

**Experimental**

## Yield

To be determined during testing.

## Required Equipment

- [Sous vide](../../equipment/sous-vide.md) and grill, or [oven](../../equipment/oven.md) and grill
- Cutting board
- Knife

## Ingredients

- Pork spareribs
- Scotch bonnet peppers or habaneros
- Scallions
- Garlic
- Fresh ginger
- Fresh thyme
- Ground allspice
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- Black pepper
- Brown sugar or molasses
- Soy sauce
- Lime juice
- Neutral oil
- Salt

## Method

1. Build a jerk marinade or paste.
2. Season the ribs and allow them to marinate.
3. Cook using sous vide or the oven until tender.
4. Finish over high heat on the grill for browning and char.
5. Serve with lime and optional hot sauce.

## Pepper Strategy

- Prefer Scotch bonnets for their fruity, floral character.
- Use habaneros when Scotch bonnets are unavailable.
- Remove seeds and membranes when less heat is desired.

## Quality Targets

- Clear allspice, thyme, scallion, ginger, garlic, and chile aroma
- Tender meat without becoming mushy
- Charred edges without a burned spice paste

## Notes

- Avoid turning this into generic sweet barbecue ribs.
- Exact marinade quantities and cooking schedule still need validation.

# Avocado Tomato Salad

A fresh, cooling salad for spicy or rich mains.

## Status

**Experimental**

## Yield

4–6 servings.

## Required Equipment

- Cutting board
- Chef's knife
- Mixing bowl

## Ingredients

- Ripe avocados
- Tomatoes
- Red onion
- Lime juice
- Cilantro
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Olive oil, optional

## Method

1. Cut the tomatoes and red onion.
2. Dice or slice the avocado shortly before serving.
3. Dress gently with lime, cilantro, salt, pepper, and optional olive oil.
4. Serve immediately.

## Quality Targets

- Avocado ripe but still holding its shape
- Bright lime acidity
- Fresh, cooling contrast without excess liquid

## Notes

- This was selected as a cooling side for jerk ribs.
- Exact ingredient quantities still need validation.

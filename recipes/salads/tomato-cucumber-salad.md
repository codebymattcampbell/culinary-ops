# Tomato Cucumber Salad

A simple, bright salad for balancing rich, braised, fatty, or warm-spiced dishes.

## Status

**Experimental**

## Yield

4–6 servings.

## Required Equipment

- Cutting board
- Chef's knife
- Mixing bowl

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 English cucumber, halved lengthwise and sliced
- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- Fresh parsley
- Fresh mint, optional
- Lemon or lime juice
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Method

1. Cut the vegetables shortly before serving.
2. Salt lightly and rest briefly if the tomatoes need help releasing flavor.
3. Dress with citrus, olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve cold or at room temperature.

## Quality Targets

- Crisp cucumber
- Juicy tomatoes that still hold their shape
- Bright acidity without heavy dressing

## Notes

- Keep the dressing light so the vegetables remain the focus.
- Exact dressing quantities still need validation.

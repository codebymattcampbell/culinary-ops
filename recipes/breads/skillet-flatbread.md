# Skillet Flatbread

A quick, no-yeast flatbread made with Greek yogurt for serving with braises, sauces, yogurt, and vegetables.

## Status

**Experimental**

## Yield

To be determined during testing.

## Required Equipment

- Mixing bowl
- Rolling pin or clean bottle
- Cast-iron skillet, griddle, or Blackstone

## Ingredients

- All-purpose flour
- Greek yogurt
- Baking powder
- Salt
- Olive oil or butter for finishing, optional

## Method

1. Mix the flour, yogurt, baking powder, and salt into a soft dough.
2. Rest the dough briefly.
3. Divide and roll into thin rounds.
4. Cook on a hot, dry skillet or griddle until browned in spots and cooked through.
5. Keep warm in a clean towel until serving.

## Quality Targets

- Flexible enough to fold or scoop food
- Browned spots without a burned exterior
- Fully cooked center without raw flour flavor

## Notes

- The no-yeast design keeps the feedback loop short enough for weeknight testing.
- Exact ingredient quantities, rest time, thickness, and pan temperature still need validation.

# SIDE - Tomato Cucumber Walnut Herb Salad

## Equipment

- Cutting board
- Chef's knife
- Mixing bowl
- Microplane or fine grater

## Meal Role

- Bright / crunchy salad for roast dinners and rich mains

## Ingredients

### Salad

- 2 ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 English cucumber, halved lengthwise and sliced
- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1/2 cup flat-leaf parsley, roughly chopped
- 1/4 cup fresh dill, chopped
- 2 tbsp fresh mint, chopped, optional
- 1/2 cup walnuts

### Dressing

- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
- 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
- 1 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 garlic clove, finely grated
- 1 tsp honey, optional

### Seasoning

- Kosher salt
- Freshly ground black pepper
- Zest of 1/2 lemon

## Method Sketch

1. Prep vegetables and herbs.
2. Whisk dressing ingredients separately.
3. Toss salad shortly before serving.
4. Add walnuts at the end so they keep texture.
5. Adjust salt, pepper, and lemon zest.

## Design Notes

- This salad was planned with chuck roast, carrots/parsnips, Yorkshire puddings, and gravy.
- Walnuts provide crunch and richness without making the salad heavy.

# SIDE - Tomato Cucumber Salad

## Metadata

- Type: SIDE
- Cuisine: Flexible / Mediterranean-adjacent
- Status: Draft
- Health Rating: Everyday
- Servings: 4-6
- Serving:
  - Calories: TBD

## Equipment

- Cutting board
- Chef's knife
- Mixing bowl

## Meal Role

- Bright / acidic / crisp side
- Useful when the main dish is rich, braised, fatty, or warm-spiced

## Ingredients

- 2 ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 English cucumber, halved lengthwise and sliced
- 1/4 red onion, thinly sliced
- Fresh parsley
- Fresh mint, optional
- Lemon or lime juice
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Method Sketch

1. Cut vegetables shortly before serving.
2. Salt lightly and rest briefly if the tomatoes need help.
3. Dress with citrus, olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper.
4. Serve cold or room temperature.

## Design Notes

- This side exists to cut through rich mains.
- Keep it simple. Do not bury it under a heavy dressing.

# SIDE - Ethiopian Cabbage (Atakilt Wat)

## Metadata

- Type: SIDE
- Cuisine: Ethiopian inspired
- Status: Draft
- Health Rating: Everyday
- Servings: 6
- Serving:
  - Calories: ~160
  - Protein: ~3 g
  - Carbs: ~22 g
  - Fat: ~7 g
  - Fiber: ~6 g

## Equipment

- Dutch oven or large saute pan
- Stove
- Cutting board
- Chef's knife

## Meal Role

- Sweet / earthy vegetable side
- Best with rich braised meats, grilled meats, or lentils

## Ingredients

- Green cabbage, sliced
- Carrots, sliced
- Yukon Gold potatoes, diced
- Yellow onion, sliced
- Garlic, minced
- Fresh ginger, minced or grated
- Turmeric
- Black pepper
- Salt
- Neutral oil or olive oil

## Method Sketch

1. Cook onion until softened.
2. Add garlic, ginger, turmeric, salt, and pepper.
3. Add carrots and potatoes and cook briefly.
4. Add cabbage and cook until tender but not mushy.
5. Adjust salt and serve warm.

## Design Notes

- The cabbage should become sweet and tender.
- Potatoes make the side more substantial but can be reduced if the meal already has bread or rice.

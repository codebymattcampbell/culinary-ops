# SIDE - Avocado Tomato Salad

## Metadata

- Type: SIDE
- Cuisine: Flexible / Caribbean dinner support
- Status: Draft
- Health Rating: Everyday / Balanced
- Servings: 4-6
- Serving:
  - Calories: TBD

## Equipment

- Cutting board
- Chef's knife
- Mixing bowl

## Meal Role

- Fresh / cooling / bright side for spicy or rich mains

## Ingredients

- Ripe avocados
- Tomatoes
- Red onion
- Lime juice
- Cilantro
- Salt
- Black pepper
- Olive oil, optional

## Method Sketch

1. Cut tomatoes and red onion.
2. Dice or slice avocado shortly before serving.
3. Dress gently with lime, herbs, salt, pepper, and optional olive oil.
4. Serve immediately.

## Design Notes

- Chosen instead of pineapple because pineapple can trigger heartburn.
- Works as a cooling contrast with jerk ribs.

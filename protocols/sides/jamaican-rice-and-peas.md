## Equipment

- Stove pot or Instant Pot

## Meal Role

- Starchy / bean-based side for jerk ribs or other Caribbean-style mains

## Ingredients

- Brown rice or white rice
- Kidney beans or pigeon peas
- Coconut milk
- Scallions
- Garlic
- Fresh thyme
- Allspice
- Bay leaf
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Method Sketch

1. Cook aromatics briefly.
2. Add rice, beans, coconut milk, water, thyme, allspice, bay, salt, and pepper.
3. Cook until rice is tender.
4. Rest covered before fluffing.

## Design Notes

- Brown rice is acceptable for family preference, though white rice is more traditional.
- Keep coconut present but not so heavy that it competes with ribs.

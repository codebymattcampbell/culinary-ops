## Equipment

- Mixing bowl
- Rolling pin or bottle
- Cast iron skillet, griddle, or Blackstone

## Meal Role

- Warm bread for scooping sauces, braises, yogurt, and vegetables

## Ingredients

- All-purpose flour
- Greek yogurt
- Baking powder
- Salt
- Optional olive oil or butter for finishing

## Method Sketch

1. Mix flour, yogurt, baking powder, and salt into a soft dough.
2. Rest briefly.
3. Divide and roll into thin rounds.
4. Cook on hot dry skillet or griddle until browned in spots.
5. Keep warm in a towel.

## Design Notes

- No yeast version for weeknight use.
- Good first bread experiment because the feedback loop is fast.

# CONDIMENT - Lemon Herb Yogurt

## Metadata

- Type: CONDIMENT
- Cuisine: Flexible / East African dinner support
- Status: Draft
- Health Rating: Everyday
- Servings: 6
- Serving:
  - Calories: TBD

## Equipment

- Mixing bowl
- Microplane or fine grater

## Meal Role

- Bright / sour / cooling condiment
- Best with braised lamb, spicy meats, flatbread, roasted vegetables, or cabbage dishes

## Ingredients

- Plain Greek yogurt
- Lemon zest
- Lemon juice
- Garlic, finely grated
- Fresh mint, chopped
- Cilantro or parsley, chopped
- Salt
- Black pepper

## Method Sketch

1. Stir yogurt with lemon zest, lemon juice, garlic, herbs, salt, and pepper.
2. Rest 10-20 minutes if possible.
3. Adjust salt and lemon before serving.

## Design Notes

- This is not meant to be tzatziki exactly.
- Its job is to cool and brighten the plate without pulling the meal fully into Greek flavors.

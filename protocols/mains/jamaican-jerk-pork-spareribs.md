## Meal Role

Pair with:

- Jamaican rice and peas
- Avocado tomato salad
- Lime wedges
- Scotch bonnet or habanero based heat at the table

## Flavor Direction

Lean into jerk rather than generic BBQ.

Key flavors:

- Allspice
- Fresh thyme
- Scallions
- Garlic
- Ginger
- Scotch bonnet or habanero
- Lime
- Brown sugar or molasses
- Black pepper

## Pepper Strategy

- Preferred: Scotch bonnet peppers for fruity, floral flavor.
- Substitute: Habaneros if Scotch bonnets are not available.
- Use peppers for flavor more than punishment-level heat.
- Remove seeds and membranes if needed to control heat.

## Ingredients

- Pork spareribs
- Scotch bonnet peppers or habaneros
- Scallions
- Garlic
- Fresh ginger
- Fresh thyme
- Ground allspice
- Cinnamon
- Nutmeg
- Black pepper
- Brown sugar or molasses
- Soy sauce
- Lime juice
- Neutral oil
- Salt

## Method Sketch

1. Build jerk marinade or paste.
2. Season ribs and rest/marinate.
3. Cook using sous vide or oven until tender.
4. Finish on propane grill for color and char.
5. Serve with lime and optional hot sauce.

## Design Notes

- Avoid ketchup-heavy BBQ sauce for this version.
- The goal is jerk identity: allspice, thyme, scallion, ginger, garlic, and Scotch bonnet aroma.
